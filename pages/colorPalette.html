Description

Green( Cal Poly, Mantis, Forest Green) : The color green is associated with nature that makes people think of growth. Mixture of two primary colors, blue, which gives the sense of security and brand trust, and yellow that represent optimism and youthfulness.It is going to be our primary color.

Brown (Sepia, Chocolate, Bistre ): Brown is natural and neutral color and it evokes the feeling of warmth, security and earthiness. We will be using it for the buttons, shadows and highlights.

Dark Orange: Orange color is associated with optimism and enthusiasm. We will be using it on our “vegetable” pages.

Azure Blue: Blue is also associated with inspiration depth, trust and wisdom. We will be using it on our” fruit” pages.
