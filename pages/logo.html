Logo

Seed Sprout's most recognizable aspect, our logo, allows users

In this page you will see how our logo is used, the "do-nots",

Vertical Logo

Design

Our logo represents our brand, ideas, and message.

The logo consists of a plant sprouting from a seed. You can also see the way the sprout has grown is in the shape of an S.

We decided to use Philosopher as the typeface of the logo as we felt its personality matched well with the style of the logo. The pointy serifs ressemble the ends of leafs, giving the font an organic but impactful look to it.

Clear Space
